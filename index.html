instructions

  1. Select a difficulty level by choosing the appropriate option.
  2. The puzzle pieces will be automatically shuffled based on the selected difficulty level.
  3. Drag and drop puzzle pieces from the side panel into the main puzzle area.
  4. Double-click on a puzzle piece to rotate it.
  5. Arrange the pieces in the main area to match the picture or pattern.
  6. If needed, use the "Shuffle" button to rearrange the puzzle pieces.
  7. Once you have solved the current puzzle, the next level will automatically be presented.
  8. Repeat the above steps for each level until you complete all the levels.
  9. Enjoy the satisfaction of completing the jigsaw puzzle!
